============================================
  GET FORM REQUESTS IN YOUR INBOX (FORMSPREE)
============================================

Do these steps ONCE. After that, every form submission will arrive
in your email. No activation link needed.

STEP 1 – Create a Formspree account
   • Go to: https://formspree.io
   • Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
   • Sign up with your email (e.g. elrichtrading2006@gmail.com)

STEP 2 – Create a new form
   • After login, click "+ New Form"
   • Form name: e.g. "ELRICH Trading Contact"
   • Email: enter elrichtrading2006@gmail.com (this inbox will receive all requests)
   • Click "Create Form"

STEP 3 – Copy your Form ID
   • On the form page you'll see an endpoint like:
     https://formspree.io/f/xjblabqd
   • The FORM ID is the part after /f/  →  xjblabqd
   • Copy that short code (yours will be different)

STEP 4 – Put the Form ID in your website
   • Open script.js and find this line:
     var FORMSPREE_ID = 'YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID';
   • Replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID with your actual ID, e.g.:
     var FORMSPREE_ID = 'xjblabqd';

   • Open index.html and find the form tag:
     action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID"
   • Replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID with the same ID, e.g.:
     action="https://formspree.io/f/xjblabqd"

STEP 5 – Save, upload your site again
   • Save both files and redeploy your site (e.g. Vercel).

STEP 6 – Test
   • Open your live site, send a test request.
   • Check elrichtrading2006@gmail.com – you should get the submission.
